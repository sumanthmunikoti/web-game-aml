Instructions

In this HIT you will be playing a multi-player negotiation strategy game. Your goal is to converse and strike deals with other players in the game using the chat feature. When the game begins you will be given "clean money", "dirty money", "Precious metals", "artwork" and "property". You buy/sell each of these commodities with other turkers  and finish the game. You're free to buy as much and sell as many times. You can play the game as much as you can and as many times. 

 

You win bonus by striking the right deal with others. You win the highest bonus by accumulating the "Clean money". The "dirty money" wins you the least bonus. The other products such as "Precious metals", "Property" and the "Artwork" will vary in price and your bonus will depend on the transactions made on the current market price. These prices change fast. 

 

Your task: 

  • Deal and negotiate buying and selling of your collection. 
  • Buy low (Buy property when Current Market Price is low) & Sell high (Sell when the Current market price for the items are high). You will get a high bonus. 
  • ALL your properties increase your bonus. Your final bonus depends on the Current Market Price for the current items you own. 
  • Keep in mind, you can finish playing the game after doing at least 5 transactions and after chatting with the users about these transactions. If we don't see your chat in our database your task will be rejected.  Irrespective of the bonus you will not get any money if you don't chat with others. 

Your HIT will be rejected if: 

  • You do not perform at least 5 transactions. 
  • If you're being dishonest. Dishonesty is when you promise to transfer money/commodity to other player and you don't do it.
  • If you're reported by others about your dishonest gameplay your HIT will be rejected. 
  • You do not use the chat. 
  • If you're abusive.
Some tips: 
  • Your ability to win the maximum bonus depends on how well you negotiate using chat.
  • BUY LOW AND SELL HIGH. 
  • As your bonus payment depends on your clean money (so will other players). So, you could sell your commodity for less of clean money and more of dirty money.
